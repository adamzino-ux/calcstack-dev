How to Use This Calculator

Enter your home price, down payment, annual interest rate, and loan term. The calculator instantly shows your monthly principal & interest payment, plus optional property tax and insurance costs.

Use the sliders to quickly explore different scenarios. Add an extra monthly payment to see how much interest you can save and how early you'll pay off your loan.

Scroll the amortization table to see a full month-by-month or year-by-year breakdown of every payment over the life of your loan.
