How to Use This Calculator

Enter your initial investment, any regular monthly contributions, and the expected annual return rate. Choose how often interest compounds and your investment horizon in years.

Click Calculate Growth to instantly see your projected final balance, total amount invested, and interest earned — broken down year by year.

Use the rate slider to quickly compare different return scenarios and see how small changes in return rate dramatically affect long-term results.
