How to Use This Calculator

Select your salary type (annual, monthly, weekly, or daily), enter your salary amount, then adjust the hours per week and weeks per year sliders to match your work schedule. Click Calculate Hourly Rate to see your equivalent hourly wage and a full pay period breakdown. Optionally enable the tax estimator to see approximate take-home pay.

The formula is: Hourly Rate = Annual Salary ÷ (Hours per Week × Weeks per Year).
