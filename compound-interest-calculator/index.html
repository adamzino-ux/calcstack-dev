How to Use This Calculator

Enter your starting principal, annual interest rate, and investment duration. Optionally add regular contributions (monthly, weekly, quarterly, or annually) to see how consistent saving accelerates growth.

Adjust the compounding frequency to match your account — most savings accounts compound monthly or daily. The yearly breakdown table shows your balance at the end of each year.
